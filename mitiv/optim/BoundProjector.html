    public abstract class BoundProjector
extends ConvexSetProjector
    Abstract class for bound projection.

    A BoundProjector can be used to implement bound constraints. Compared to a ConvexSetProjector, it has also the ability to project the gradient, that is to generate (the opposite) of a feasible steepest descent direction.

      • projectGradient

        public void projectGradient(Vector x,
                   Vector g,
                   Vector gp)
        Project the gradient of bounded variables.

        Produce a direction gp such that its opposite -gp is the steepest feasible descent direction.

        Alternately, g can be an ascent direction and the result gp is such that -gp is the feasible direction which minimize the Euclidean norm of g - gp.

        Parameters:
        x - - The variables (must be feasible, that is within the bounds).
        g - - The gradient at x.
        gp - - The result, i.e. the projected gradient. The operation can be done in-place, with g and gp the same vector.
        Throws:
        IncorrectSpaceException - if its arguments do not belong to the correct vector space.
