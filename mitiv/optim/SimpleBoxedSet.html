        public abstract void setLowerBound(Vector vec)
        Set the lower bound of the variables. This method sets the component-wise lower bounds of the variables. If the bound value is null, the variables will be considered as being unbounded below. Any element of vec equal to Double.NEGATIVE_INFINITY or Float.NEGATIVE_INFINITY will be considered as being unbounded below.

        vec - - A vector with the new lower bounds.
