      • BY_STS_OVER_STY

        public static final InverseHessianApproximation BY_STS_OVER_STY
        Approximate the inverse Hessian by a scaling by the Rayleigh quotient of the mean inverse Hessian in the direction of the latest gradient difference.

        This approximation is a scaling by the latest value of:

        γ'k = skt.sk/skt.yk

        This approximation was introduced by Shanno and works well with limited memory quasi-Newton methods and conjugate gradient methods with monotone line searches.

      • BY_STY_OVER_YTY

        public static final InverseHessianApproximation BY_STY_OVER_YTY
        Approximate the inverse Hessian by a scaling by the reciprocal of the Rayleigh quotient of the mean Hessian in the direction of the latest step.

        This approximation is a scaling by the latest value of:

        γ''k = skt.yk/ykt.yk

        This approximation works well with nonmonotone line search.
