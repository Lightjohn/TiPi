      • getGradientThreshold

        public double getGradientThreshold()
        Query the gradient threshold for the convergence criterion. The convergence of the optimization method is achieved when the Euclidean norm of the gradient at a new iterate is less or equal the threshold: 
            max(0.0, gatol, grtol*gtest)
        where gtest is the norm of the initial gradient, gatol grtol are the absolute and relative tolerances for the convergence criterion.
        Returns:
        The gradient threshold.
        See Also:
        #setAbsoluteTolerance}, {@link #setRelativeTolerance}, {@link #getAbsoluteTolerance}, {@link #getRelativeTolerance}.
