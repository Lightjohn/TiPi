        public double[] getWorkspace()
        Retrieve the internal workspace used for in-place FFT. The methods forwardFFT() and backwardFFT() are applied in-place to the internal workspace array of the instance of this class. This workspace is large enough to store 2*N values with N the number of elements of the convolution.
        Returns:
        The internal workspace used for in-place FFT.
