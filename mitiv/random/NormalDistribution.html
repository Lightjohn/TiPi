      • nextDouble

        public double nextDouble()
        Returns the next pseudo-random normally distributed double value. Based ont the polar method of G. E. P. Box, M. E. Muller, and G. Marsaglia, as described by Donald E. Knuth in "The Art of Computer Programming," Volume 3: Seminumerical Algorithms, section 3.4.1, subsection C, algorithm P. Note that it generates two independent values at the cost of only one call to Math.log and one call to Math.sqrt.
